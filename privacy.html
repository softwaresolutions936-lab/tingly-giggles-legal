Tingly Giggles – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 November 2025

1. About this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tingly Giggles (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information in connection with the Tingly Giggles tool and related services (the “Service”). The Service is primarily used by a single creator to plan and publish short-form ASMR-style content to platforms such as TikTok and YouTube.

This policy is intended to give a clear, simple explanation and does not replace any privacy terms provided by TikTok, YouTube, or other platforms you use.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Account identifiers from platforms you connect: for example, IDs or usernames that TikTok or other platforms provide when you authorise the Service to post on your behalf.
  • Access tokens and technical credentials: tokens or keys that allow the Service to upload content to your TikTok or other connected accounts, subject to the permissions you grant.
  • Content and metadata you provide: such as video files, titles, descriptions, hashtags, and other details used to publish your ASMR content.
  • Basic usage information: for example, logs indicating that an upload was attempted, succeeded, or failed, and simple error messages that help diagnose issues.

The Service does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, health information, or government-issued IDs.

3. How we obtain information

We obtain information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you: when you configure the Service, connect your accounts, or provide content, titles, and descriptions.
  • From third-party platforms: when you authorise the Service via TikTok, YouTube, or other platforms, those platforms provide access tokens and limited account information needed to perform actions you request (such as uploading a video).

4. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Generate ideas and metadata for your content (for example, titles and captions);
  • Upload and publish videos to platforms you connect, on your behalf and with your permission;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems (for example, understanding why an upload failed).

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Legal basis (where applicable)

Where data protection laws require a legal basis (for example, in the UK or EU), we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the functionality you expect from the Service;
  • Legitimate interests: to operate and improve the Service, keep it secure, and prevent misuse.

6. Sharing of information

We may share information:

  • With the platforms you connect: for example, when publishing a video to TikTok, we send the video and associated caption/metadata to TikTok via their APIs.
  • With service providers: where necessary to host or operate the Service (for example, cloud infrastructure or logging providers), subject to appropriate safeguards.
  • When required by law: if we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations or to respond to lawful requests.

7. Data retention

Information is kept only for as long as necessary to operate the Service and achieve the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

You may revoke access tokens or permissions from your TikTok or other connected accounts at any time using those platforms’ settings. Doing so may prevent the Service from being able to post on your behalf.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

  • Access to certain information we hold about you;
  • Correction of inaccurate information;
  • Deletion of certain information;
  • Restriction or objection to certain processing; and
  • Portability of certain information.

Since Tingly Giggles is a limited, creator-focused tool, requests can typically be handled by contacting the creator directly using the contact details provided in the app or social profiles.

9. Children

The Service is intended for use by adults managing their own social media accounts. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe information about a child has been collected in connection with the Service, please contact us so that it can be reviewed and, where appropriate, removed.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to help protect information processed in connection with the Service. However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted or stored.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Service after any changes means you acknowledge the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how Tingly Giggles handles information, you can contact the creator of the tool using the details shared in the app or on the associated social media profiles.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general information only and is not legal advice. If you require legal certainty about your obligations, please consult a qualified professional.
